AURORA SYSTEMS
Employee Handbook
Section 4.2: Remote Work Policy

Effective Date: January 2026
Owner: People Operations


OVERVIEW
========
Aurora Systems supports remote work as a flexible arrangement that benefits
both the employee and the company. This policy defines who is eligible for
remote work, how to request it, and the expectations that come with it.


ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
====================
To be eligible for a remote work arrangement, an employee must meet ALL of
the following conditions:

  1. Tenure
     The employee must have completed at least six months of continuous
     employment with Aurora Systems. Employees in their initial probation
     period are not eligible.

  2. Role designation
     The role must be designated as remote-eligible by the department head.
     Customer-facing roles in the Showroom and Field Operations divisions
     are not remote-eligible due to in-person service requirements.

  3. Performance
     The employee must hold a "Meets Expectations" or higher rating on
     their most recent performance review. Employees on a performance
     improvement plan are not eligible until the plan is closed.

  4. Workspace
     The employee must have a private, secure home work location with
     reliable internet connectivity of at least 50 Mbps download speed.


APPROVAL PROCESS
================
Eligible employees may apply for remote work by submitting a Remote Work
Request form through the People Portal. Each request is reviewed by:

  - The employee's direct manager
  - The employee's department head
  - The HR Business Partner assigned to that department

Decisions are returned within ten business days of submission. Denials
must include a written reason and may be appealed once to the Head of
People Operations.


ARRANGEMENT TYPES
=================
Approved remote work arrangements fall into one of three categories:

  - Fully Remote: The employee works from their home location full time.
    Office visits are limited to quarterly company gatherings.
  - Hybrid 3-2: Three days in the office, two days remote each week.
  - Hybrid 2-3: Two days in the office, three days remote each week.

All arrangements are reviewed annually as part of the performance cycle.


EQUIPMENT AND STIPEND
=====================
Aurora Systems provides every approved remote employee with a company
laptop, an external monitor, a keyboard, and a mouse. A one-time stipend
of five hundred US dollars is available toward home office setup, payable
upon submission of itemized receipts within ninety days of arrangement
approval.


WORKING HOURS AND AVAILABILITY
==============================
Remote employees are expected to be available during their team's core
hours, defined as 10:00 to 16:00 in their local time zone. Outside of
core hours, employees may flex their schedule provided they meet weekly
deliverables and attend all scheduled meetings.


DATA SECURITY
=============
Remote employees must use company-provided equipment for all work tasks,
must keep the laptop's full-disk encryption enabled, and must connect
through the corporate VPN when accessing internal systems. Use of personal
devices for company work is prohibited under any circumstance.


POLICY OWNERSHIP AND REVIEW
===========================
This policy is owned by the People Operations team and is reviewed
annually. Questions or proposed changes should be directed to
people-ops@aurorasystems.example.
