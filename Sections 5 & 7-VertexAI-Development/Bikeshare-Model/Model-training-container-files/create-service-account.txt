1. Go to IAM & Admin → Service Accounts in the GCP Console
2. Click Create Service Account
3. Service account name: vertex-training-sa
4. Service account ID: auto-fills as vertex-training-sa
5. Description: "Service account for Vertex AI custom training jobs"
6. Click Create and Continue
7. On the Grant this service account access to project step, add these roles one by one:
   - Vertex AI User (lets the SA run training jobs)
   - Storage Object Admin (read hour.csv, write model.joblib to your bucket)
   - Artifact Registry Reader (pull the training image)
   - Logs Writer (write training logs)
8. Click Continue → Done
